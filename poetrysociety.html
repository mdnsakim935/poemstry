Assurance

The nights seems long,
I got sleepless nights,
I got none today,
I ain't assured of tomorrow.
All along we've been struggling,
Struggling to make ends meet,
At times i loose hope today,
Yet i ain't assured of tomorrow.
We got responsibilities,
Responsibilities here and there,
We failed yesterday,we wail today,
Yet we ain't assured of tomorrow.
We will never rest,
We will always fight today,
With the hope of a better tomorrow,
Yet we ain't assured of tomorrow.
We fight to live good,
We are thou leaving someday,
We are gonna live today,
Yet we ain't assured of tomorrow.
